The Practitioners Of Chinese Wisdom
After nearly ten years of research and accumulation, Initial Science Research Institution was formally established in 2016. The word "Yuan Zhen" is easy to understand, the begin and the reality. As a practitioner of Chinese cultural wisdom, we believe that Chinese cultural wisdom is the most original and true science of the Chinese nation, the greatest treasure of our Chinese nation, and the cornerstone of the unique core algorithm of Initial Science Research Institution.
After 18 months of combing and focusing, Initial Research Institute officially changed our name to Initial Science Value Investment, focusing on the exploration and investment of high-quality start-ups. We are convinced that early equity investment has unlimited opportunities and possibilities. It is our responsibility and mission to find and nurture the outstanding entrepreneurs of China's future and to accompany them to realize the value of life, corporate value and social value.
We adhere to our stable development rhythm and people-oriented origin investment philosophy, low-key and steady, and pursue more solid and long-term planning and development. We are not eager to combine with the Chinese cultural wisdom system for the future of equity investment in start-ups. We believe in the philosophy of quality, obey the principle of investment that is not heavy, not heavy, and work slowly. There is only one theme, value investment.
